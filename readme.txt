Detects toxic and spam links on your website. Identifies external links that may harm your SEO and visitor's trust.

== Description ==

Outbound links, also called external links, direct visitors from pages on your website to another site. External links are a great metric for determining the popularity of the domain names. This is why hackers and spammers are trying to get as many links as possible to their black hat projects. When they insert links on your website that directs customers to their sites it sends positive signals to search engines and can boost a pageâ€™s rank in search results. 

When someone inserts links on your site that directs users to suspicious sites you can get penalized by Google. Our plugin helps detect and remove links that have been inserted without your permission. It scans images, iframes, javascript codes and detects if someone inserted a suspicious link or redirect.

** Main Features **

* Detects links in CMS files
* Detects links in images
* Blacklist of suspicious sites
* Fast scan

** Benefits **
Our plugin allows you to detect harmful external links and prevent your site from getting penalized by Google. With one click you will get a report of all outbound links from your site. You can easily remove those links with a few clicks of a mouse button.

== Installation ==

1. Upload all the files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
